Semantic rules

Rule 01 · Pairing

Mint & Tangerine for parallel items

When showing two parallel things (A vs B, recommended vs alternative), one gets Mint and the other gets Tangerine. Never both Mint.

Rule 02 · Negative states

Coral Red, sparingly

Coral Red is reserved for true negatives — exceeds threshold, deficit, risk, blocker. Don't use for normal accents.

Rule 03 · Cards

Forest Card on Deep Pine, with Mint borders

Cards always have rounded 10–12px corners and a 1px Mint border. Switch to Tangerine border for secondary / parallel card.

Rule 04 · Highlighted card

Mint fill, Ivory text

The one card you want the eye drawn to gets a solid Mint fill and Ivory text. Don't fill more than one card per row.

Rule 05 · Headings

Micro caps in Mint, tracked-out

Section headings are short, all-caps, 11–12px, weight 700, letter-spacing 0.16em, coloured Mint. Always preceded by a 24px Mint rule for that "Affy look."

Rule 06 · Typography

Inter, not Manrope

Use Inter as the default. It's slightly more geometric than Manrope and signals "this is Affy" without being incongruous next to Okra material.
